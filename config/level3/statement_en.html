  The Goal

You somehow managed to survive first 2 rounds against Maester. He is proud of you and angry at the same time. Now it's time to show you his true powers.

  Rules

In this round Maester will cast various spells of certain power. If the spell's value is below 100 you are able to ATTACK Maester. If the spell's value is equal to or higher 100 you have no other choice but use DEFEND spell order to deflect his spells. However this time you will have to defend yourself by repeating DEFEND spells power times in sequence. For example if power = 2 and spell = 150 then you have to print DEFENDDEFEND

 
Victory Conditions
Do not make single mistake
 
Lose Conditions
Mistake has been made

  Game Input

Input

Single line: power and spell separated by space

Output
A single lineATTACK or power times DEFEND based on game rules
